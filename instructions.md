# Instructions: Getting Started with the AI Executive Hub

Welcome to your **AI Productivity System | Executive Hub**. This dashboard is the operational core of the *AI Productivity Systems for Busy Professionals* course. Follow these instructions to set up, use, and scale your personal AI ecosystem.

---

## 1. Initial Setup
1. **Launch**: Open the `index.html` file in your preferred web browser. 
2. **Persistence**: Your progress, notes, and prompts are saved automatically to your browser's local cache. 
   - *Note: If you clear your browser cache, please use the "Export" button regularly to maintain a backup of your progress.*
3. **Synchronization**: If you are using this across multiple devices, use the **Export** button to save your `hub-data.json` file and store it in a secure location (e.g., cloud drive or local backup).

## 2. Core Workflows
### The Learning Loop
*   **Track Progress**: Mark lessons as "Done" as you complete them. The progress bar will update your **Optimization %** in real-time.
*   **Capture Insights**: Use the integrated `textarea` under every lesson to record key takeaways. These notes are critical for your "Weekly Audit."

### Prompt Engineering Suite
*   **Build**: Use the **Prompt Engineering Suite** to test new instructions. 
*   **Deploy**: Click "Deploy to Library" to save your prompts locally. This turns your hub into a proprietary "Prompt Database" that you own.

### The 15-Minute Weekly Audit
*   Every week, use the sidebar input to define your high-leverage goals.
*   Once the week concludes, click **"Reset Efficiency"** to clear the deck and begin your next cycle with a clean dashboard.

## 3. Extending Your Ecosystem
This hub is designed to work in tandem with the resources provided by **[Digital Selling Global](https://do-essential.net/)**:

1.  **Course Integration**: Follow the modules in the *AI Productivity Systems for Busy Professionals* course. Use this hub to perform the tasks suggested in each module.
2.  **Asset Scaling**: When you download new templates or Prompt Libraries from the [Digital Selling Global Store](https://do-essential.net/), use this hub's "Prompt Engineering Suite" to categorize and store those assets for immediate access.
3.  **Collaborative Workflow**: Use your exported data to identify which areas of your AI implementation need more attention, then revisit the corresponding course material.

## Troubleshooting
*   **Data Not Saving?** Ensure you are not browsing in "Incognito" or "Private" mode, as these sessions often clear `localStorage` upon exit.
*   **Visual Glitch?** If the interface appears broken, refresh the page. This forces a reload of the CSS/JS files and refreshes the layout.
*   **Support**: For advanced modules or to acquire additional tools, visit the [Digital Selling Global eBook Store](https://do-essential.net/).
