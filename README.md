# AI Productivity System | Executive Hub

The **AI Productivity System | Executive Hub** is a premium, enterprise-grade dashboard designed to track mastery, manage AI workflows, and optimize productivity for busy professionals. 

This software is the official companion tool developed in collaboration between:

*   **[AI Productivity Systems for Busy Professionals](https://do-essential.net/b/ai-productivity-systems-for-busy-professionals)**: The foundational online course providing the framework for agentic workflows and AI-driven success.
*   **[Digital Selling Global eBook Store](https://do-essential.net/)**: The exclusive source for high-leverage digital assets, business templates, and the Professional AI Prompt Library.

## Key Features
*   **Curriculum Mastery**: Track your progress through the *AI Productivity Systems* course modules with real-time optimization metrics.
*   **Note-Taking & Reflection**: Integrated, persistent storage for your insights within every module.
*   **Prompt Engineering Suite**: An enterprise-grade "Sandbox" to version, store, and deploy your proprietary prompts.
*   **Weekly Audit Trigger**: A dedicated tool for defining high-leverage goals and resetting your weekly efficiency stats.
*   **Data Portability**: Full support for exporting your progress, notes, and saved prompts locally.

## Deployment & Privacy
This system is designed for secure, local-first operation. It utilizes browser-based `localStorage` to ensure your notes, progress, and prompts remain private and persistent on your machine.

1. **Deploy**: Host this dashboard via GitHub Pages or run locally using a simple web server.
2. **Sync**: Use the "Export" functionality to back up your *Executive Hub* data as a JSON file.
3. **Extend**: Visit the [Digital Selling Global store](https://do-essential.net/) to find new modules, prompt libraries, and tools to feed into your hub.
